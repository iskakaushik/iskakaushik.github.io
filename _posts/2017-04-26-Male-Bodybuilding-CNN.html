Male Bodybuilding CNN

Part - 1

It is quite common to see bodybuilding models. But how often does one come across a bodybuilding deep learning model? This is an attempt to build just that. A classifier that can detect if an image is of a "shirtless" male bodybuilder.

Getting lot of training data is hard. Keras recognized that and even has a tutorial that walks you through how you can be clever about training a relatively awesome model with just a few hundred images. For the images themselves I used insta #bodybuilding. Thanks, world!

Note

1. Since we will be working with a limited set of images, we should try and make our model as simple as possible. The way I think of it -- if a model is complex, if acquires a lot of information about your training set, and can be prone to overfitting.

2. Keras tutorial talks about ways to modulate the entropy or the amount of memory the model has about our training data. Some of these are L1/L2 regularization, or Dropout (where we do not allow a layer to see too many images of the same kind?). I have not studied them yet, but I get a sense for the high level idea though.
